About Amazing support 365, a subsidiary of Crown world ltd

At Amazing Support 365, we understand that every individual and organization has unique needs when it comes to care and support. Established with a mission to provide outstanding care services, we have grown into a trusted name known for professionalism, compassion, and dedication.

Who We Are

Amazing support 365, a subsidiary of Crown world ltd is a team of experienced and passionate professionals committed to delivering top-tier services in healthcare, childcare, cleaning, and advocacy. From support workers to translators, our diverse team is united by a shared vision of enhancing lives and empowering communities.

Our Values

  • Compassion: We treat every client with empathy and respect.
  • Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in everything we do.
  • Integrity: We are honest, transparent, and accountable.
  • Diversity: We embrace and celebrate individual differences.

Our Vision

To be a global leader in care and support services, renowned for innovation, reliability, and compassion.

Our Commitment

We are dedicated to making a meaningful difference by tailoring our services to meet the unique needs of each client. Whether it’s providing personal care, fostering child development, or advocating for rights, we are here to help.
