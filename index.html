Our services

Care Consultancy

Whatever the type of service you have, our team of experts work with you to ensure that every aspect of your care service is working to the best of its ability.

CQC Consultancy

The Delphi task force work in collaboration with your team to give advice, guidance and ensure every aspect of your service adheres to the guidelines set by the CQC.

Mock CQC Inspections

Regardless of whether your current rating is Outstanding or Inadequate, our consultants have the knowledge and experience to help you maintain or dramatically improve your CQC Rating.

Interim Management

Having someone leading your team is a crucial part of any business. Our interim management solution gives you reassurance that you service is in good hands whilst you find the right person.

Investigations

We understand the sensitive nature of serious investigations and work collaboratively with your team to give an impartial perspective and produce a thorough report based on our findings.

CQC Registrations

The process of CQC registration can seem incredibly daunting. Our team can manage the entire process for you, giving you time to focus on the other aspects of setting up your new service.

Crisis Management

Transforming services to improve financial and operational performance, or even in some cases, stopping them from being shut down, is what we do. We have the knowledge and experience to guide you at every stage.

Marketing & Occupancy

We understand the difficulties, and stress, of finding and engaging new residents. This is why we have developed strategies to help increase the profile of your care home, and ultimately your occupancy and revenue.

Acquisition & Due Diligence

We can support you through the complete acquisition process both financially and operationally leaving you free to focus on running your business.

Investment & Exits

Delphi offer a full partnership service working alongside you in implementing and managing the day-to-day operations of the service whether you’re looking to enter the world of care or exit it.

Quality Audit & Policies

Delphi can work in conjunction with you to produce a comprehensive action plan to help improve the quality of service given whilst also ensuring all policies are compliant and up to date.

Care Software

Our software has been designed by care providers, for care providers. Whether it’s care management software or data visualisation to help you make decisions for your business, we have the solution for you.

Utopi ESG Technology

Utopi is the specialist ESG Technology platform for the Health and Social Care Real Estate sector.
